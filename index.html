SPELL STORM

Cast spells with your hands & voice

HOW TO PLAY

🛡️ SHIELD CD: 4s

Open hand + say "shield". Destroys projectiles near your palm.

"shield"
🔥 FIRE CD: 3s

Point fingertip ON a target + say "fire". Ignites with magic flame.

"fire"
⚡ LIGHTNING CD: 5s

Point + say "lightning". Bounces off targets & walls. More bounces with higher level.

"lightning"
☠️ POISON CD: 6s

Say "poison" to activate. Toxic trail wherever your hand moves. Costs HP. Recast or use another spell to stop.

"poison"
❄️ FREEZE CD: 8s

Open hand + say "freeze". Freezes all nearby targets for 5s — they stop moving and take +30% damage. Scales with level.

"freeze"
💚 HEAL
Say "heal" to collect green drops from killed targets.
🔥 LEVEL
Level up after multiple kills. Higher level = stronger spells.
☠️ BOSS
A boss appears every 5 waves. The next wave won't start until it's dead. Killing it fully heals you and drops bonus loot.
🔴 PROJECTILES
Blue → orange → red. Shield when red, before they vanish.
⚠️ BROWSER
Voice requires Chrome or Edge. Allow camera & microphone.
